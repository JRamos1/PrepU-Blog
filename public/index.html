What is Prep U?

Prep U is a community of ambitious young students and professionals that are looking to connect with Alumni to gather information and exchange idea on what school and program can help them best prepare them to their future and dream career.
Let us know your interests, ambitions and goals and our team of experts will match you with alumni and young professionals whom have degrees or are currently working in that field.


I am a HS student or I am thinking about Grad School: Help!

Prep U wants to help you making the right choices for your future. We will pair you with someone who has been in your shoe. "I want to work in consulting. Is this school right for me?", "I am thinking about going to law school. Is it worth it?"
Our alumni & young professional community will help you think this through. They will tell you their experience, pros and cons about the school they sent to and what helped succeed in the field their are in. SIGN UP


I graduated and would love to mentor the new generation!

Prep U will give you the opportunity to help, guide and mentor the new generation. We will pair you with a student that is thinking about going to the school you went, wants to follow similar career path or has similar interests.
You will be able to share your experience, lesson learned and advices from your successful journey, and help them make the right choices so they can achieve their goals! SIGN UP
